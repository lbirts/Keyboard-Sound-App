How To:

Record a sound

To add a sound to a letter, or edit a letter's sound, click one of the letters below. To record, hold down the r key, on your keyboard, and release to stop recording.

Play the sound

To play the sounds tap the corresponding, a, s,d,f, or g key, on your keyboard.
